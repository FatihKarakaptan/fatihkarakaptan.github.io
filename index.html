John Mccarthy

JOHN MCCARTHY'nin Hayatı

  John Mccarthy was born in 1927, in Boston/USA He grew up in Boston and Lost Angeles. He studied Mathematics at California Institute of Technology and he graduated in 1948. He received his PhD in Mathematics in 1951, at Princeton University. He invented ''Lisp Programming Language'' and ''Artificial İntelligence''and he has been a pioneer in the multi-user timesharing systems. He was awarded the Turing Prize in 1971. He worked in MIT and Stanford University and he retired in 2000. He died in 2011. He called “ the Father of AI(artificial intelegence) and timesharing systems”.
